## Overview

When you connect an MCP client to Atlas, the OAuth flow asks you to approve specific permissions. These scopes control what your AI assistant can do.

## Available scopes

| Scope | What it allows | Required for |
|-------|----------------|-------------|
| `openid` | Basic identity verification | All connections |
| `profile` | Read your name and account details | All connections |
| `email` | Read your email address | All connections |
| `discovery:read` | Read discovery runs and their results | Viewing past research runs |
| `discovery:write` | Create and manage discovery runs | Running new research |
| `entities:write` | Create and update catalog entries | Adding or editing entities |

## Default scopes

Most MCP clients request `openid`, `profile`, and `email` by default. The `discovery:*` and `entities:write` scopes are requested when you use tools that require them.

## Revoking access

To disconnect an MCP client and revoke its access:

1. Sign in to [Atlas](https://atlas.rebuildingus.org)
2. Go to **Account** settings
3. Under **Connected apps**, find the client and click **Revoke**

After revoking, the client will need to re-authorize on its next use.
