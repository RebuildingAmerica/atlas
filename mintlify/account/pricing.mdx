## Overview

You never pay to *see* civic data. You pay to *work with* it professionally.

Browsing, searching, and reading the Atlas directory is free for everyone, forever. Paid plans unlock professional research tools, API access, and team collaboration.

## Plans in detail

### Free

For anyone curious about civic work in their community.

Browse and search the full Atlas directory. Run up to 2 research runs per month. Save one shortlist with up to 25 entries. Use the public API at 100 requests per hour.

No sign-up required to browse. Create an account to save shortlists and run research.

### Atlas Pro — $5/month

For individual researchers who need a professional workspace.

Everything in Free, plus:
- **Unlimited research runs** — no monthly cap
- **Unlimited shortlists and notes** — organize your research however you want
- **CSV and JSON export** — take your data with you
- **1 API key** with 1,000 requests per day
- **MCP and OAuth access** — connect Claude, Copilot, Gemini, and other AI assistants

Annual billing: **$48/year** (2 months free).

### Atlas Team — $25/month base + $8/seat

For newsrooms, nonprofits, and research teams who need to coordinate.

Everything in Pro, plus:
- **Shared workspace** — collaborative shortlists and notes across your team
- **Unlimited API keys** with 10,000 requests per day per key
- **Watchlists and monitoring digests** — get notified when Atlas discovers new entries matching your criteria
- **Slack integration** — pipe digests and alerts to your team channels
- **SSO (SAML / OIDC)** — enterprise single sign-on
- **Up to 50 members**
- **Priority support**

The base price covers the workspace. Each member beyond the first is one seat. Annual billing: **$250/year base + $80/seat/year**.

### Atlas Research Pass

For short-term projects that need Pro capabilities without a subscription.

- **30-day pass**: $9 one-time
- **7-day pass**: $4 one-time

Grants Pro-level capabilities for the pass duration. When it expires, your workspace reverts to Free. Shortlists, notes, and saved research remain accessible read-only until you subscribe or buy another pass.

<Tip>
If you know you'll need access for more than a month, Atlas Pro at $5/month is more cost-effective than buying passes.
</Tip>

## Discounts

Atlas offers 40–50% discounts for people doing civic work:

- **Journalists** — verified working journalists and media professionals
- **Grassroots nonprofits** — organizations with limited budgets
- **Civic tech workers** — people building tools for public benefit

To apply, visit the [discount request page](https://atlas.rebuildingus.org/request-discount) and provide verification details. Applications are reviewed within a few business days.
